Method

First Step

Boil pasta according to package direction. (10 minutes)

Second Step

Cook bacon in a pan to render fat. Strain bacon bits and set aside. (5 minutes)

Third Step

Sauté garlic in the same pan. Pour water and NESTLÉ® All Purpose Cream. Stir in cheese and simmer. Season with MAGGI® MAGIC SARAP® and pepper. (10 minutes)

Fourth Step

Strain spaghetti and toss in the sauce. Transfer on a serving plate and top with bacon bits. Serve immediately. (5 minutes)
